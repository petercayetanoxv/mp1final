Dias Shop Portal Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Dias Shop Portal ("the Portal") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. By using the Portal, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information when you register an account, make a purchase, or communicate with us. Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information, including browsing history, IP address, device information, and cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: We use your personal information to provide you with services, process orders, and facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers on the Portal. Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, purchases, updates, and promotions. Improve User Experience: We analyze non-personal information to enhance your experience on the Portal, including customizing content and improving our website.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. However, please be aware that no data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

5. Sharing Your Information

Sellers: We share your information with sellers to complete your orders and facilitate communication between buyers and sellers. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us with payment processing, order fulfillment, and other services.Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, respond to legal requests, and protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect non-personal information about your browsing activities. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

The Portal may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can manage your account information and preferences by logging into your account. To exercise these rights, or if you have any privacy concerns or questions, please contact us at diasshop@support.com.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of authority.
