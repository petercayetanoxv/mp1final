How to buy ML Diamonds?

  1. Click here to visit the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Top Up page
  2. Enter your MLBB user ID and Zone ID.
  3. Select the value of Diamonds you wish to purchase.
  4. Complete the transaction and the diamonds will be instantly delivered to your Mobile Legends account.

You can also send Diamonds to your family and friends by typing in their user ID and Zone ID.

How to buy VALORANT Points?

  1. Click here and input your Riot ID.
  2. Select the amount of Points you want to purchase.
  3. Select the payment method that is most convenient for you.
  4. Complete the transaction and the VALORANT Points will be instantly delivered to your VALORANT account.

You can also send VALORANT Points to your family and friends by typing in their Riot ID.

How to Buy Steam Wallet Codes?

Adding funds to your Steam Wallet is easy, safe & convenient at Codashop. Simply follow the steps below, and the Steam Wallet Code voucher will be sent in seconds.

  1. Click here to get started.
  2. Select the Steam Wallet denomination you wish to purchase.
  3. Choose your preferred payment method.
  4. Complete the payment.

Once payment is complete, you will instantly receive the Steam Wallet Code on the payment confirmation page and via email.
